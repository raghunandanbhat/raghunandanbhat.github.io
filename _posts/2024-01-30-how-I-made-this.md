This is a newer vserion of my github pages site made using Jekyll Hacker-Blog theme by [Ashish Chaudhary](https://ashishchaudhary.in/). There is a older version of my github pages under the [`main`](https://github.com/raghunandanbhat/raghunandanbhat.github.io/tree/main) branch, but it's not in use anymore.

This served from [`gh-pages-jkl`](https://github.com/raghunandanbhat/raghunandanbhat.github.io/) branch. You can have versions of your site under same repo and just change the publishing source to view differnt versions.

There's nothing special about this site, same old content with new look. I hope to publish more blogs here.
